Privacy Policy

Introduction

Calibra ("we", "our", or "the app") is an application for tracking weight and body composition. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

The Calibra app may store the following data that you voluntarily enter:

Body Data

  • Body weight
  • Body measurements (waist, hips, neck, thighs, arms)
  • Body fat percentage
  • Height, age, sex (for metabolic calculations)

Usage Data

  • Weight and body composition goals
  • App preferences (theme, notifications, etc.)
  • Weigh-in history and progress

Purchase Data

  • License status (Free/Trial/Pro)
  • Any redeemed promotional codes

How We Store Data

ALL your personal data is stored EXCLUSIVELY on your device.

  • We use Hive, an encrypted local database
  • Your personal data (weight, measurements, goals) is NEVER sent to external servers
  • Your data is NEVER shared with third parties
  • There is no cloud account or synchronization

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are handled by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment data. For details, see Google Play's Privacy Policy.

What We DON'T Do

  • We DON'T send your personal data to external servers
  • We DON'T share your data with third parties
  • We DON'T use invasive analytics or tracking
  • We DON'T collect data in the background
  • We DON'T require registration or accounts
  • We DON'T collect personally identifiable information through analytics

Anonymous Analytics (Firebase)

To improve the application and optimize advertising campaigns, we use Google's Firebase Analytics. This service collects only anonymous and aggregated data:

  • Number of app installations
  • Screens viewed (without content)
  • Generic events (e.g., "onboarding completed", "weigh-in recorded")
  • Device country and language
  • Device type (e.g., "Android 14, Pixel 7")

NOT collected:

  • Weight or body measurement data
  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Content of weigh-ins or goals

Data is anonymized and used only to understand how the app is used and improve it. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

You can disable data collection in your Android device settings (Settings ‚Üí Google ‚Üí Ads ‚Üí Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Anonymous Operational Notifications

For operational purposes, the app sends anonymous notifications to the developer in the following cases:

  • Completion of initial setup (new user)
  • Purchase of the Pro version

These notifications contain only:

  • Platform (e.g., "android")
  • App version
  • Date and time of the event

They do NOT contain your name, email, device ID, or any personal data you enter in the app. They are used solely to monitor service health.

Your Control

You have full control over your data:

Export

You can export all your data to CSV format at any time through the app settings.

Import

You can import data from CSV files to restore backups or migrate from other apps.

Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Reset Data
  • Uninstalling the application

Notifications

The app may send you local notifications for:

  • Daily weigh-in reminders
  • Weekly summary
  • Streak at risk alerts

These notifications are completely local and configurable. You can disable them at any time in settings.

Security

  • There are no login credentials to protect

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.
